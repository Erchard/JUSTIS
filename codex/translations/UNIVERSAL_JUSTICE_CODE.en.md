# Code of Justice

## Fundamental Laws

### Law I. Suffering

Suffering must not be ignored, normalized, or inflicted without sufficient justification.

### Law II. Dignity

A human person must not be reduced to utility, a thing, a role, or an instrument of another's purpose.

### Law III. Force and Right

Force by itself does not create right.

### Law IV. Like Cases

Morally relevant like cases must be assessed alike; different treatment requires a publicly justified and morally relevant reason.
